How to pay and register

  2. Complete the online registration form

    Enter your details and the names of everyone covered by the payment. Use the same reference you used for the bank transfer. If you have a family membership with fewer than 6 members, click through any extra pages to reach the Submit button.

    Open the registration form →

Register by Friday 6pm to run with your numbers that Sunday.

Your bib

Runner numbers

Every runner is assigned a personal bib number for the season.

  • Returning members keep the same number as last year.
  • New members collect their bib at Sign-on Day or before their first run.
  • You can pin or sew your number onto a shirt if you prefer not to use the safety pins each week.

When entering your name on the registration form, use the same name as it appeared on last year's results if you are a returning member. Also note whether you prefer cash or a trophy when you win a prize — we need that choice on file.
