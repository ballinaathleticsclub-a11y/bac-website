Join Ballina Athletics Club

One form, one payment, and you are covered for the full season. Fill in your details below and we will have your bib numbers ready to collect at Shelly Beach.

▶

25 Sundays at Shelly Beach

Every race day from late March to September. Come when you can, skip when you can't. Your membership covers the whole run.

$

Lucky draws every week

Two $20 cash prizes every Sunday. Your Finish Number goes into the draw automatically. You just need to be there.

★

Monthly and annual prizes

Trophies and cash for top point-scorers each month and at the season finale. Trophy or cash — your choice on the form.

☼

Barbecues and community

A few hundred locals, all ages, on the sand. The Ted Batterham Memorial BBQ in July. The presentation BBQ in September.
