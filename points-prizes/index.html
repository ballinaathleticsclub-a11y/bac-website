How scoring works

Weekly points

Points are awarded each Sunday based on your placing within your age group:

Place in age groupPoints
1st20 pts
2nd15 pts
3rd10 pts
4th8 pts
5th or more5 pts
Time trial / early start5 pts
Volunteer8 pts

Points accumulate across each four-week period. The highest scorer in each age group wins a monthly prize, provided they have 30 points or more for that month. Once you win a monthly prize, you are not eligible to win the same monthly prize again that season — which means everyone gets a turn eventually.

Annual prizes

Annual prizes go to the top 3 point-scorers in each age category across the full season. To be eligible for an end-of-season prize you need 100 points or more.

If you run different distances across the season, you get 5 points per week toward your best distance's total for the weeks you ran a shorter distance. For example, 10 weeks at 3K and 2 weeks at 1K gives you an extra 10 points toward your 3K annual total. This applies to the annual total only, not the monthly scores.
