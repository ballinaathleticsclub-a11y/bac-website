Annual event

Ted Batterham Memorial BBQ

A family run & barbeque held every year in memory of Ted Batterham - a valued member, past Secretary, and friend of the club.

2026 event

Sunday 26 July 2026

Races run at the usual time from Shelly Beach, followed by a family BBQ at the carpark 150m north of Belle General Café.

9:00am · Shelly Beach, Ballina

On the day

  • ✓ Watches must not be worn during the run
  • ✓ No iPods or similar devices during the run
  • ✓ Family BBQ follows at the carpark

Trophies

Awards on the day

Open trophy

Ted Batterham Trophy

Awarded to the athlete who finishes at the time nearest to their handicapped time.

Junior trophy

Daley's Gourmet Meats Trophy

For junior members 16 years and under in the 1K, 3K, and 6K events who finish closest to their handicapped time.

About Ted

A valued club member

Ted Batterham was a dedicated volunteer and friend who gave years of service to Ballina Athletics Club.

  • 1987 Vice President
  • 1988–92 Secretary
  • 1992 Handicapper

This annual event keeps his memory alive in the club he helped build.
